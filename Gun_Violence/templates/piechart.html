Pie Chart: Gun Violence (2014-2019)

This pie chart displays the type of gun violence incidents which resulted in either death or injuries during the years of 2014 through 2019 across the United States of America. The categories included:
Mass Shootings
Murder Suicides
Defensive Use
Unintentional Shootings
Home Invasions

The data displayed shows how home invasion leads to the most incidents of gun violence. It typically leads to about 35% of yearly gun shootings. Defensive shootings follow close behind home invasions for the second leading cause of gun shootings. This trend remained steady throuhgout the duration of this study. One intresting spike did occur in the number of unintentional shootings in the years of 2016 and 2017, it was never able to topple defensive shootings but it was certainly a large portion of yearly gun shootings.
